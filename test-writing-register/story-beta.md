# The Forgotten Library

In a village where books were scarce, a young girl discovered a locked room filled with dusty volumes. The village elders had forgotten it existed. She began reading by candlelight, and soon shared what she learned with other children. Over time, the library grew into a gathering place where knowledge flowed freely.

When she became old, travelers came from distant lands to study in the library she had revived.

**Moral:** Preservation begins with remembering what was never truly lost.
